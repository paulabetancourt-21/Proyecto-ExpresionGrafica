Admission & Aid

Join Us at Princeton

A Vibrant Community Open to All

Princeton is a vibrant community that seeks to attract and support students of all backgrounds and interests. We are a leader in ensuring admitted students can afford college, offering one of the strongest need-based financial aid programs in the country.

Affordable for All

We are committed to making a Princeton education affordable to all. Our generous financial aid program allows undergraduates to graduate from Princeton debt free.

Graduate students also benefit from significant University support and other funding resources, including fellowships, assistantships, and grants that cover tuition and living expenses.

Need-Based Aid Only Princeton meets 100% of demonstrated financial need for all admitted students. We do not offer merit scholarships — every dollar of aid is need-based, ensuring that financial circumstances never prevent a qualified student from attending.

Paths to Princeton

Undergraduate

Undergraduate Admission

Princeton's undergraduate program is one of the most selective in the world. Applications are reviewed holistically, considering academic achievement, personal character, extracurricular contributions, and potential to contribute to the University community. Princeton offers both Single-Choice Early Action and Regular Decision application rounds.

How to Apply Applications are submitted through the Common Application or QuestBridge. Required materials include transcripts, standardized test scores (optional), letters of recommendation, and Princeton-specific essays.
Graduate

Graduate Admission

The Graduate School is at the heart of Princeton's teaching and research mission. We prepare graduate students for distinguished careers across all disciplines. Students from all backgrounds are encouraged to apply to Ph.D. and master's programs.

Funding for Graduate Students Most Ph.D. students receive full funding packages that include tuition, a stipend for living expenses, and health insurance for the duration of their program.
International

For International Students

Princeton is a diverse community that welcomes students and faculty from around the world. International undergraduate applicants are evaluated on the same criteria as domestic applicants and are eligible for the same need-based financial aid program.

Visa & Immigration Support The Davis International Center provides dedicated support for visa applications, immigration advising, cultural adjustment, and community programming for international students and scholars.

Questions About Financial Aid?

Princeton's Office of Financial Aid is committed to providing clear and personalized guidance throughout the application and enrollment process. Prospective students and families can request an estimated aid package before applying, and current students have a dedicated financial aid counselor assigned to support them each year. Financial aid packages are renewable annually based on continued demonstrated need.

Visit Us

A visit to our beautiful campus will give you a good idea of what Princeton has to offer. Tours and information sessions are available throughout the year, led by current Princeton students.
